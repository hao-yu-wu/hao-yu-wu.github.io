Haoyu Wu

Hi, I am Haoyu Wu (吴浩宇), a Ph.D. candidate at Stony Brook University, supervised by Prof. Dimitris Samaras. I received my bachelor's degree from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen.

My research focuses on computer vision and deep learning, and I have a deep passion for AI-powered content creation. My expertise includes 3D reconstruction, image/3D/video generation, and diffusion models.
